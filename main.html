Summary

The Matrix is a 1999 sci-fi action film that follows the story of a computer hacker named Neo who discovers that the reality he lives in is a simulated world created by machines. He joins a group of rebels to fight against the machines and free humanity from the illusion. The film explores themes of artificial intelligence, reality, and freedom, all set in a visually groundbreaking world.
